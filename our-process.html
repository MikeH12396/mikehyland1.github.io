Our customers base is made up of three main market sectors:

'Food to Go’ which includes ready to eat sandwiches, salads, ready meals, and breakfast offers.
Quick Service Restaurants in both the UK and central Europe.
Wholesale Distributors supplying into pub chains, coffee chains, hotels, and event caterers.

We have state of the art microwave lines which create strong flavour profiles and can accurately control the crispness of the texture of the bacon with no greasy residue on the product. We also use hot air technology to cook our breakfast bacon range. Using both technologies we can guarantee slice counts and help to control costs in your business.

We believe in working together, and all cooked bacon orders are prepared to our customers’ own specifications in our ultra-modern factory. We adhere vigorously to the highest standards in sourcing, preparing and distributing food products. We have a fully integrated HACCP food safety system and we are BRC grade AA certified. The company operates a fully integrated traceability system which uses full transparency and control over all of our materials in and out of our process. Product quality is a key focus of the entire team at Dew Valley Foods and it is at the core of how we operate. Strategic planning and product innovation are at the heart of our success, supported by a Research & Development Programme to build on our leadership in advanced technology.

We closely follow and analyse food trends to ensure we are at the cutting edge of what the consumer wants and we can react quickly to key trends. We are totally customer-orientated, with a dedicated NPD team and an extensive investment programme designed to accommodate major customers without prejudicing capacity. All processes are overseen by our highly experienced and enthusiastic management team. Our state of the art NPD facilities allow us easily create customer specific solutions and demonstrate these to our customers.
